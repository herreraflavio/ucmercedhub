    UCM Hub is now live on the App Store.

    Visually explore and navigate UC Merced campus in real-time. The iOS app helps students, faculty, and visitors discover campus events faster with an interactive map built for quick exploration.

    • Interactive event mapping across campus
    • Tap markers for instant event details and room information
    • Date filtering for today, this week, and upcoming events
    • New Map Feed for switching between specialized campus maps
